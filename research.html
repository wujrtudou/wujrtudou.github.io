Research

My research lies at the intersection of statistical learning, semi-supervised inference, and computational statistics, often motivated by applications in energy systems, climate and environmental science, and engineering.

1. Statistical Learning under Complex Data Conditions

  • Semi-supervised learning with partially labelled and unlabelled data.
  • Informative and favourable missingness mechanisms in mixture models.
  • Robust estimation for heavy-tailed noise and contaminated observations.

2. Computational Statistics & Optimisation

  • Efficient optimisation for high-dimensional and non-convex statistical models.
  • Stochastic, iterative, and evolutionary optimisation algorithms.
  • Swarm-based meta-heuristics for model training and hyperparameter tuning.
